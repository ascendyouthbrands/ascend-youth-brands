Who We Are

A dedicated partner for youth enrichment business owners.

Ascend Youth Brands is a holding company that acquires and partners with exceptional youth enrichment businesses across North America. We provide the infrastructure, capital, and expertise that allow owners and operators to scale their impact without losing what makes their programs special.

We understand this industry because we are passionate about it. Whether you run a dance school, a gymnastics gym, a music school, a swim program, or an after-school tutoring center, we know that the hardest part of your job isn’t teaching or coaching. It’s running the business.

We take that burden off your plate. You focus on inspiring young people. We focus on building the business around you.

Everything you need to grow, in one partner.

We remove the operational stress so you can gain access to best-in-class resources and focus on what you love.

Industries We Serve

Youth enrichment, broadly defined.

We partner with businesses across the full spectrum of youth enrichment, united by a shared mission of making a real difference in young lives.

Sports & Athletics
Youth sports academies, gymnastics gyms, cheer programs, training facilities, multi-sport clubs, and martial arts programs. Any business helping young people build physical confidence, discipline, and teamwork.

What We Look For

Is your business a fit?

We seek out businesses that combine genuine community impact with strong fundamentals and a founder who cares deeply about what they’ve built.

01
Proven Revenue & Profitability
We partner with businesses that are already working, with demonstrated profitability and a clear financial track record. We are not a startup investor; we want proven businesses with real revenue and happy customers.
02
Strong Community Presence
Businesses with loyal families, strong word-of-mouth, and genuine roots in their community. The kind of program where parents tell their friends and kids don’t want to leave.
03
Recurring or Enrollment-Based Revenue
We love businesses with predictable revenue driven by memberships, seasonal enrollments, or subscription programs. These models create stability and clear visibility into future performance.
04
Growth Potential
Whether through additional locations, new program offerings, or expanded enrollment, we look for businesses with room to grow and a model that can scale without losing what makes them special.
05
A Founder Who Cares
We work best with owners who are passionate about their mission and open to partnership. Whether you want to stay on and grow with us, or transition to a new chapter, we respect and support both paths.
Our Sweet Spot
We focus on founder-owned businesses in the youth enrichment space that have outgrown their current infrastructure. You’ve built something real, and we bring the resources to take it further.

Our Process

A clear path from first conversation to long-term partnership.

We’ve built our process to be transparent, efficient, and respectful of your time as a founder and operator.

1
Introductory Call
A no-pressure conversation to understand your business, your goals, and whether there’s a partnership worth exploring further.
2
Letter of Intent
If there’s mutual fit, we move quickly to a non-binding LOI outlining a proposed structure within 1–2 weeks.
3
Due Diligence
A focused review of your financials and operations, typically 45 to 90 days. We keep it efficient and always share our findings openly.
4
Partnership & Growth
We close the transaction and immediately get to work bringing resources, marketing, capital, and operational support to your business.
